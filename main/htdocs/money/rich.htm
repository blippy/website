Measuring wealth

In the late 2000's, in terms of net disposable wealth, there exist 1.94m Brits who are emerging affluent (£50,000-£99,000), 1.6m who are affluent (£100,000 - £499,999), and  0.10m  who are high net worth individuals (£500,000+)

In 2009, Merrill Lynch - Capgemini World's Wealth Report 2009 defined HNWIs (High Net Worth Individuals) as those who hold at least US$1m in financial assets, and UHNWI (Ultra-High Net Worth Individuals) as those who have at least US$30m

Placings

Signs that a placing are underway in a way that will disadvantage you:
  • The house broker will normally be prohibited from publishing research just before a placing. So watch for a raft of notes from "independent" research houses such as Edison and Hardman.
  • The CEO will do a raft of interviews with folks like Proactive Investors and in resources present at Proactive or Minesite or similar forums.
  • The PR folks will call in favours and you will see the shares tipped in magazines and puffed up in certain parts of the press.
  • There will be a string of upbeat RNS releases. Most companies that are loss making seem to issue an RNS once a month with good news anyway. But when it becomes even more frequent than usual watch out.

UK Stock Challenge 2016

Yearly entries

WIG (Winning Income & Growth) Screen was my first port of call, as it has high returns with low volatility. DTG seemed too good to pass up, and IGG was recommended by John Kingham. I wanted to choose one share from each sector, but figured that doubling up in consumer defensive was acceptable. It seemed as though it would make a good barbell strategy in the style of Nassim Taleb: get some good safe companies (SBRY, TET), and combine them with a risky one (PAF).

Spinoffs

Edgar

Forms:
  • 10-12B registration statement that companies must file when they issue new stock in a spinoff
  • 10-12B/A is an amendment.
  • 10-12G contains info about the num of shares, their par value, and owenership
  • 10-K annual report
  • 10-Q quartely filing of accoungs
  • S-4 an exchange offer o occurs when a company offers to exchange securities that it provides for similar securities at less demanding terms. This is often done in an attempt to avoid bankruptcy. Submissions of this form are closely watched by investors who attempt to make quick gains from M&A activity
  • SC 13D - form required when a person or group acquires more than 5% of any class of a company's shares. The information must be disclosed with 10 days of the transaction.
  • SC 13G - shorter form used to report a party's ownership of stock that is over 5% of the company.

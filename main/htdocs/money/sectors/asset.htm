Asset managers

In this book written by Martin J. Whitman 'Value Investing: A Balanced Approach' he says that for financial institutions like broker/dealers and other money managers you should buy when the price is lower than tangible book value plus 2% to 3% (I think he means to 5%) of assets under management.

Dart Group PLC is a Leisure Airline, Package Holidays and Distribution and Logistics company specializing in the operation of scheduled and charter flights by Jet2.com to leisure destinations throughout Europe; the provision of ATOL protected package holidays by its tour operator Jet2holidays, and the distribution of fresh produce, temperature-controlled, and ambient products to supermarkets and wholesale markets throughout the United Kingdom. The Company operates in three segments: the Leisure Airline, Package Holidays and Distribution & Logistics. Leisure Airline consists of the Company’s scheduled and charter airline, Jet2.com. Package Holidays, consists of the Company ATOL protected tour operator, Jet2holidays; and Distribution & Logistics consists of the Company logistics company, Fowler Welch.

03-Jul-2014 Share pricing

Current share price 217p. Target prices based on various models:
  • Dividends: over last decade, mean dividend was 15.41p, and dividend yield was 3.9%. Therefore, assuming everything gets back to normal, its share price should be 395p (=15.41/0.039)
  • PBV: Current PBV is 0.64, and it should be worth at least 1. Implied price: 217 * 1 / 0.64 = 339p
  • PE: ROE: current 4.3%, decade mean 11.68%. PE now 11.04, decade mean 8.45. Therefore fair value assuming normalisation of PE and ROE is 217 * (8.45/11.04) * (11.68/4.3) = 451p

Greggs plc is a United Kingdom-based bakery retailer. The Company sells a range of fresh bakery goods, sandwiches and drinks in its shops. As of January 2, 2010, Greggs plc had 10 regional bakeries with a fleet of 375 vehicles. It also had 90 instore bakeries. As of January 2, 2010, the Company opened 49 new shops and closed 39 and had a total of 1,419 shops. Its subsidiaries include Charles Bragg (Bakers) Limited, Greggs (Leasing) Limited, Thurston Parfitt Limited, Greggs Properties Limited, Birketts Holdings Limited and J.R. Birkett and Sons Limited.

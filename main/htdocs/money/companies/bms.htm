Braemar Shipping Services plc provides a range of marine related services. The Company services industries, including ship owning, oil and gas, mining, insurance and financial services. It operates in four operating divisions: Shipbroking, Technical, Logistics and Environmental. Braemar Seascope Limited provides specialized shipbroking and consultancy services to international clients. Braemar Steege Holdings Limited provides specialist loss adjusting and other expert services to the energy, marine, power and other related industrial sectors. It has offices in London, Houston, Miami, Singapore, Calgary and Mexico City. Cory Brothers Shipping Agency Limited provides port agency, freight forwarding and logistics services within the United Kingdom, Europe and Singapore. Braemar Howells Limited provides pollution response and advisory services, primarily in the United Kingdom, for marine, tank storage and rail operations and has developed an international presence in West Africa.

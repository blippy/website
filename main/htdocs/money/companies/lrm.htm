The Company is engaged in the provision of trading, valuation and risk management systems and regulatory compliance systems to the financial markets. The Company operates in two segments: Regulatory Compliance software and Risk Management and trading software. Regulatory Compliance software is for regulatory, anti-money laundering and compliance systems to financial markets. Risk Management and trading software provides trading, valuation and risk management systems to the financial markets. During the fiscal year ended March 31, 2011, it launched its LISA product. The Company's products include COLLINE, OBERON, (STB-) Reporter, FIRMAMENT, LISA and MIS Reporting Suite. COLLINE is a collateral management product. OBERON is an integrated, multi-currency system for the trading and risk management of interest rate derivatives and inflation derivatives. (STB-) Reporter is a regulatory and compliance software product.

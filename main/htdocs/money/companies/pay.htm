PayPoint plc is a United Kingdom-based specialist payments company, with operations in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Romania, France, United States of America and Canada. The Company, along with its subsidiaries, is engaged in providing clients with specialist consumer payment transaction processing and settlement. It processes transactions and collects payments on behalf of the United Kingdom and Ireland utility and customer service organizations, and there are 22,600 convenience retail outlets using PayPoint's terminals. At a PayPoint outlet, consumers are provided with a one stop shop for making cash payments made by the PayPoint's clients. It also provides other services to retail outlets. PayPoint.net provides secure credit and debit card payments services for Web merchants. PayPoint Romania provides electronic mobile top-ups, scratch cards and a bill payment service to consumers. On March 9, 2010, the Company acquired Verrus Mobile Technologies Inc and Verrus UK Limited.

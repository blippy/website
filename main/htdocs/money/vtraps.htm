Value traps

Hollanders article

From an article dated 05-Jul-2011. Here are some tips to recognize a value trap:
  • Avoid shares involved in fraud or that have inaccuracies in the accounts
  • Be careful of companies with an overly optimistic management
  • Be careful with companies in highly regulated industries
  • Avoid stocks that have reduced, or eliminated, their dividend
  • Avoid stocks with high debt
  • Be careful of companies in a highly competitive sector
  • Avoid stocks that have dropped in price due to outdated products or services
  • Only look at companies that make a profit
  • Be careful with cyclical companies, their appreciation seems the cheapest when they are the most expensive and visa versa.
  • Look at the behavior of insiders, are they buying or selling?
  • Despite the fall in price, is the underlying strategy of the company is still intact?

Title: My approach to investing - by John Rosier - 31-Dec-2013

  • mid and small cap preferred
  • Projected PE < 15, PEG < 1.5
  • Earnings upgraded
  • Growing dividends
  • Avoid companies with high levels of debt
  • Companies hitting new highs
  • Ideas from: valuation screens, directors buying/selling
  • Don't catch falling knives

    Setting stop losses

  • At what price would it look like the trnd/momentum had changed
  • 50dMA and 200dMA often act as support
  • Don't use automatic stop losses

    2013 Mistakes

  • Selling too early
  • Not being disciplined with stop losses

Terry Smith: I've returned 17pc a year by following these three rules

Article appeared in The Telegraph on 10-Nov-2014.

  1. Require ROCE ≥ 15pc.10pc or less is a machine for destroying value
  2. Check that profits translate to cash. Compare like with like: Ensure that Net cash flow to net profit or the operating cashflow to the operating profit is at least 90pc. Otherwise, don't bother.
  3. How does the company make its returns?. Look for companies that make a large number of everyday repeat predictable transactions. Avoid businesses that make their money on large one-off occasional things (e.g. property, movies)

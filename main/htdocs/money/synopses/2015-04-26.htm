Title: Stock plummet decision matrix - 04-Feb-2015

Stockopedia ran an article on Mauboussin's compilation of stocks that had declined 10% or more in a single day. Although his original criteria had momentun, quality and valuation, I have condensed it down to two criteria: momentum and valuation.

Value
Momo Cheap Neutral Expensive
High Keep Sell Sell
Neutral Buy Keep Sell
Weak Buy Keep Buy

E.g. an expensive share with high momentum should be sold.

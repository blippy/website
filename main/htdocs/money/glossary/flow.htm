FR: Flow ratio

Measures how asset light or heavy a company is. 
       (current assets - (cash + short-term investments))
FR =   --------------------------------------------------
       (current liabilities - short-term debt)
  • FR < 1.5 : company is 'asset light', and is a really good figure
  • FR > 3.0 : company is 'asset heavy', and is a warning sign

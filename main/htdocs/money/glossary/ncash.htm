NCASH: Net Cash

The Digital Look website is a reliable source for a company's balance sheet. NCASH is "net interest-bearing cash", and is typically used to calculate EV. 
NCASH :=  cash & cash equivalents - short term debt - long term debt
Net Debt is just the negative of NCASH: 
ND := -NCASH

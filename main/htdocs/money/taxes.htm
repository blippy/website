Foreign dividends

FTCR = Foreign Tax Credit Relief. See SA-106.

JEY - Jersey - Dividends paid to foreign shareholders have no withholding tax.

SGP - Singapore

USA - United States America

JEY, SGP and USA all have DTA (Double Taxation Agreement)

JEY, SGP: ineligible for FTCR

USA: eligible for FTCR

OEIC rebates

Rebates given to advisers by OEICs do not have to be declared (xlink):
Where a fund manager pays a financial intermediary amounts such as percentage of their annual management charge (AMC) or annual commission ... it will not be taxable as an annual payment.

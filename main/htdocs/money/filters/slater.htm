Slater stock filter

A growth screen

Criteria - 18 May 2012

Use the following filters:
  • Market capitalisation of less than £500m (elephants don't gallop).
  • PEG less than 0.75.
  • P/E less than 20.
  • EPS growth greater than 15%.
  • Dividend yield greater than 1%.
  • Cash flow per share/eps greater than one.
  • One-year relative strength greater than zero.
Source: Great Investors: The Jim Slater Approach - Motley Fool

Criteria - 16 Aug 2014

  • Substantially increasing EPS over the past 2/3 years
  • Managers and broker forecasts confirming the trend will continue
  • PEG < 1
  • Cashfow per share regularly in excess of EPS
  • Strong balancd sheet
  • Positive relative strength. Weakness can be due to a few peiple being aware of imminetn bad news
  • No substantial selling of shares by directors, especially chief executive and finance director
  • Don't fuss about dividend yield
Source: Jim Slater: 50 years on, my formula still works - The Telegraph

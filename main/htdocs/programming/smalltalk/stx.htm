Smalltalk/X

I use Stx to denote Smalltalk/X. Notes about constructing GUIs. The equivalent action for VisualWorks under Stx: 
btnClicked
       | lbl txt |

       txt := (self componentAt: 'EntryField1') contents.
       lbl := self componentAt: 'Label1' .
       lbl labelString: txt .

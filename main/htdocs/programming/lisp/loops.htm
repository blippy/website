Loops

I'm collecting a list of loop idioms that might be of use in some instances.

(loop for bytes-read = (r-s b f) then (r-s b f)
 while (plusp bytes-read)
 summing bytes-read)

while-type loops

(let ((i 0))
  (loop if (< i 10) 
	do (print i) (setf i (1+ i))
	else do (return)))

gives (compressed onto one line):
1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 NIL

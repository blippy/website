ffmpeg

Speed up video: ffmpeg -i input.mkv -an -filter:v "setpts=0.5*PTS" output.mkv. -an turns off audio. xlink

Convert to 720x256 video, reduce volume a lot, skip the first 5 seconds of video, and only take 1 second of video:

ffmpeg -ss 00:00:05 -i static.mkv -t 00:00:01 -vf scale=720x576 -af "volume=0.1" static.mpg

WTV -> MP4

WTV files stutter in VLC, so you are advised to convert it to MP4. 
ffmpeg -i in.wtv -acodec libmp3lame out.mp4
A 1h5m WTV is 708M, and converts to a 310M MP4 file. AAC is the preferred audio codec for MP4, but it is an experimental codec; hence the use of libmp3lame. As of Dec 2015, AAC is no longer experimental in ffmpeg .

sudoers

It is very easy to give a specific user passwordless sudo privileges: 
echo "mcarter ALL=(ALL) NOPASSWD: ALL" | sudo tee /etc/sudoers.d/mcarter

USE=`whoami` ; echo "$USE ALL=(ALL) NOPASSWD: ALL" | sudo tee /etc/sudoers.d/$USE

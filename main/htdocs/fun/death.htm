Death Note

Volume 1
  1. Rebirth: Light discovers the Death Note
  2. Confrontation: Light kills Lind L Tailor, an imposter of L.
  3. Dealings: L realises that Kira is connected with the police. Light is tailed, and is informed by Ryuk of this fact. Ryuk offers Light the 'Shinigami eyes' in exchange for half his remaining lifespan. This would enable Light to learn the stalker's name, and thereby kill the stalker by having his name written in the Death Note.
  4. Pursuit: Light refuses Ryuk's deal. He tests the rules of the Death Note to learn of its limitations. Light boards a bus, together with his stalker. A criminal hijacks the bus, brandishing his gun. The stalker relveals to Light that his name is Raye Penber, an agent for the FBI. The criminal is manoeuvred into touching a piece of the Death Note. He can see Ryuk, and empties the bullets into Ryuk, to no effect. He flees the bus in terror, and is struck and killed by a passing car.
Volume 2
  1. Tactics: On a train, Light coerces Raye Penber into revealing the names of all 12 of the FBI agents investigating the Kira case. Light uses the Death Note to kill all of the FBI agents, including Raye.
  2. Unraveling: Light comes in contact with Naomi Misori, Raye Penber's fiancee. She reveals that she worked out that Raye came in contact with Kira on the hijacked bus.
  3. Overcast: Light kills Naomi.
  4. Glare:
    Light: I really can't count on you, can I?
    Ryuk: You got that right.

    L suspects Light, and instigates surveillance on Light. Light is aware of this, looks through girlie mags, and utters the famous line "I'll take a potato chip, and eat it"

Volume 3
  1. Encounter: Light and L start university, and meet for the first time.
  2. Doubt: Tennis match. Soichiro, Light's father, has a non-fatal heart attack due to stress.
  3. Assault: Misa and Rem make first appearance. Misa has Shinigami eyes.
  4. Love
Volume 4
  1. Confession: Light meets Misa
  2. Friend: Rem agrees to kill L
  3. Wager: Misa is taken into custody. Renounces ownership of Death Note
  4. Decision: Light renounces ownership of notebook. Incarcerated. So is Soichiro
Volume 5
  1. Execution: Misa's notebook given to gangster by Rem. Killings continue. Sham execution of Misa and Light. Misa released. Light shackled to L. Yotsuba group introduced
  2. Ally: New building. L recruits thief and conman
  3. Matsuda: Matsuda gatecrashes Yotsuba
  4. Makeshift: New title sequence
Volume 6
  1. Performance: Rem touches a piece of the Death Note. Higuchi is identified as the Yotsuba kira
  2. Guidance: Matsuda makes a TV appearance
  3. Frenzy: Higuchi cornered
  4. Revival: Light and L touch the notebook, allowing Light to kill Higuchi.
Volume 7
  1. Silence: Rem kills Watari and L
  2. Renewal: Light takes control of the investigation
  3. Abduction: Near and Mello make first appearance. Near heads SPK. Mello kidnaps Takimura and Sayu Yagami
  4. Impatience: Soichiro exchanges the Death Note for his daughter. Mello kills Near's team. Sidoh makes first appearance.
Voulme 8
  1. Father: Soichiro killed
  2. Justice: Light locates near's HQ
  3. Transfer: Mikami makes first appearance, and starts killing people with Death Notes
  4. Selection: Takada makes first apearance as "voice of kira". Matt makes first appearance, but just plays video game
Volume 9
  1. Scorn: Mikami is observed killing a perp
  2. Vigilance
  3. Malice: Matt's only appearance. Create's smokescreen. Takada kidnapped. Matt killed. Takada kills Mello. Takada dies
  4. 1.28: Meeting in a warehouse
  5. New World: Mikami commits suicide. Light dies/
